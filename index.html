This form is intended for reporting non-emergency maintenance, please call 919-996-3420 if the problem is:
  • an EMERGENCY
  • for elevator problems
  • for janitorial service
  • for door entrances such as keys, locks, and fobs
  • not listed in this form
  • for a facility not listed
An EMERGENCY is described as: a broken waterline with great amounts of water being lost; no electricity that compromises safety or programming; no heating or cooling that compromises programming; and any other situation that may result in an unsafe or hazardous environment for patrons or staff.
